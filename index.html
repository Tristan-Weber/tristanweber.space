Research

My studies focus on understanding how the evolution and habitabilty of planets are affected by the space environment. With toolsets from planetary science, space physics, engineering, statistics, and data visualization, I use spacecraft data and numerical models to analyze planetary evolution.

Teaching

One of my greatest passions is teaching. As a graduate student I designed the curriculum for a summer course entitled "The Search for Life in the Universe", and taught the course as instructor of record for two summers. I'm eagerly awaiting my next chance to enter into a classroom, and am in the process of planning curricula for courses in astronomy, physics, programming, and scientific data visualization
